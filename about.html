About the Artist

My name is Juan Lino Perez Jr. Some refer to me as Jay or Jay Lino. I am originally from Edinburg, Texas.
I moved to San Antonio, Texas in 2005 to help plant a new branch of our ministry that begin in Mission, Texas. My
interests and hobbies include graphic design, freehand calligraphy, charcoal sketching, oil painting, photography and
videography. I reside in San Antonio with my wife and daughter.
